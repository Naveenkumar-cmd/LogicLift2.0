Quick Answers

Before You Reach Out

How soon can you start on my project? ▼
We typically onboard new clients within 3–5 business days of signing. For urgent projects, we have priority slots available. Book a call to discuss your timeline.
Do you work with non-profit or startup organizations? ▼
Yes! We work with businesses of all sizes. We also have special pricing packages for early-stage startups and non-profits. Mention it in your inquiry.
Do you offer a free trial or audit before committing? ▼
Yes. Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute discovery call. For existing Zoho users, we offer a paid Zoho Health Audit that gives you a full optimization report — with no obligation to hire us for implementation.
What information should I prepare before the call? ▼
It helps to have a rough idea of: (1) your current tools and processes, (2) the biggest operational challenges you face, and (3) your team size and main departments. Don't worry if you don't have all of this — we'll guide the conversation.
