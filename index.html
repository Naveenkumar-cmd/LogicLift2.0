FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical Zoho implementation take? ▼
Most Zoho CRM or Books implementations take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. Enterprise-level projects with custom integrations may take 6–10 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline in our scoping call.
Do you provide training for our team after implementation? ▼
Absolutely. Every engagement includes a handover training session. We also offer ongoing training retainers for teams that want deeper mastery of Zoho tools.
Can you migrate our data from our existing CRM or accounting software? ▼
Yes. We handle data migration from tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, Tally, QuickBooks, and spreadsheets into Zoho with full data integrity checks.
What is your pricing model? ▼
We offer flexible pricing: fixed-scope project pricing, hourly consulting, and monthly retainer plans. Book a free 30-minute discovery call to get a custom quote based on your requirements.
Do you work with businesses outside India? ▼
Yes! We work with clients across India, UAE, UK, USA, and Southeast Asia. All our engagements are remote-friendly with dedicated communication channels.
